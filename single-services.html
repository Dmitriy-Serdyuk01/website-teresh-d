Criminal law

About practice

Protection of the rights and interests of persons in criminal proceedings is one of the priority areas of my activity. I understand how important freedom and protection from law enforcement arbitrariness and unlawful prosecution are to my clients. My lawyer's experience and practice allow you to effectively protect your rights at all stages of the criminal process.

I suggest

- Legal consultations: Study of documents, legal analysis of the situation and formation of a legal position on the case.
- Identification of risks: Assessment of the risks of prosecution.
- Preparation of procedural documents: High-quality drafting of complaints, statements, motions and other necessary documents.
- Defense at the stage of pre-trial investigation: Representation of your interests in pre-trial investigation bodies, the prosecutor's office and in courts of all instances.
- Protection during searches and interrogations: Ensuring legality and protection of your rights during investigative actions.
- Cancellation of seizure of property: Appeal of seizure of property and its return.
- Organization of examinations: Preparation and support of necessary examinations.
- Protection of the suspect: Providing protection at the stage of pre-trial investigation, including during the selection of a preventive measure.
- Representation in court: Defense of the accused in courts of all instances.
- Reconciliation support: Assistance in the organization and legal support of reconciliation with the victim in criminal proceedings.
- Representation of witnesses and victims: Protection of the rights and interests of witnesses and victims in criminal proceedings.
